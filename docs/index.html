Game Introduction
UNLUCKY CO. is filled with excitement and suspense, it's not just a game; it's also an innovative financial ecosystem where you can immerse yourself and enjoy the dual thrill of gaming and investment.
1. Each round of the game ends randomly after reaching 10 people. After the end, all players will be randomly divided into two groups. The half with the smallest total amount becomes the unlucky ones, and the other half becomes the lucky ones.
2. Unlucky ones become permanent shareholders of Unlucky Co., and the entire recharge amount becomes company shares, adding an equivalent value to the prize pool.
3. Lucky ones receive 88% of the prize pool, Unlucky Co. shareholders receive 8% of the prize pool, and community operations receive 4% of the prize pool.
